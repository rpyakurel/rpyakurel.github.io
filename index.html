Who I am

My journey into the world of data started in February of 2020 when I joined Thinkful’s Data Analytics program. But my curiosity was awakened with my high school sociology class where I read Emile Durkheim’s classic “Suicide: A Study in Sociology”. The work he did collecting, analyzing data, reaching objective conclusions to describe social phenomena was illuminating and changed how I see the world. Now, with an arsenal of data analytics tools under my belt, I am excited and actively searching for new opportunities.
